Delete Account

We're sorry to see you go. Please follow the instructions below to delete your account.

  1. Ensure you are logged in to your account.
  2. Navigate to your account settings page.
  3. Scroll down to the "Delete Account" section.
  4. Click the "Delete Account" button.
  5. You will be prompted to confirm your decision. Please read the information carefully.
  6. If you are sure, click "Confirm" to permanently delete your account.

Warning: This action is irreversible. Once your account is deleted, all your data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.
